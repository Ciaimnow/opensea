    The Bridged is a unique collection of art created by world renowned artists Antoni & Marc Tudisco. Each NFT offers its holders premium access to Kiraverse, a multiplayer third-person shooter game where gamers across the globe play to compete, and earn achievements to unlock or rent characters, skins, weapons, and more!

    The Bridged is comprised of 8 avatars. These are the "Bridged Camarillas", which are available to mint for a limited time. Each mint will reveal into 1 of the 5 Camarilla characters after the Open Edition mint has concluded. The remaining 3 avatars, known as the "Bridged Sentients", can only be obtained by owners of the Camarillas through a "Burn and Redeem" event hosted by Kiraverse.

    For more info on the Burn & Redeems go to the FAQ section of our drop page.
